In my younger years, success was synonymous with financial prosperity and a life untouched by worries about essentials. However, my perspective has evolved over time. Equipped with programming skills, my current passion centers around leveraging code to contribute to a secure global future. I am an advocate for ensuring universal access to fundamental needs. Food and health for every child on Earth!

My journey into the world of Computer Science started through the Harvard University's CS50 program on EDX Online. This experience stands out as one of the most transformative events in my life. Having dedicated over 15 years to a career in customer service, the realization that a second path awaited me was both surprising and exhilarating. It turns out, there was an untapped passion capable of eliciting an ecstatic feeling, a revelation that reshaped my professional trajectory.

Beyond the screen, my world is all about my beautiful son, whose mind is as beautiful. He is my inspiration and imagination in human form. We dream together every day. And when I'm not in mom mode, I'm up for a game of online chess. Challenge me if you dare!
